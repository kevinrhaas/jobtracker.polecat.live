Why JobTracker

Everything the FileMaker & spreadsheet era was missing.

Built from the real agency workflow — requested → in progress → review → approved → delivered — with the polish of a modern app and the speed of running right in your browser.

A real job workflow

Managed statuses with sensible transitions, aging indicators for stuck jobs, rush & priority flags, revision rounds and a lightweight approval flow.

See it your way

A delightful list with pill filters and saved views, a drag-and-drop Kanban board by status, and a calendar by due date. One click switches the lens.

Know your numbers

Live KPIs — active by status, due this week, overdue, average cycle time, throughput per month and on-time delivery — plus simple workload visibility per person.

Bring your data

A guided import wizard for your Excel/CSV, JSON, or Microsoft Forms export — with column mapping, validation preview, duplicate detection and an error report.

Never miss a date

Due dates, in-hands dates and milestones with overdue flagging. Auto-generated, never-duplicate job numbers you can search anywhere.

Yours & private

Invite-only, admin-token access. Data lives in your browser with full export. Six themes (ADA & Polecat, dark/light/system), history, undo, and a joyful, accessible design.

How it works

From request to delivered in one place.

Intake a request

Log a new job by hand or import a batch from your spreadsheet or a Microsoft Form. It gets an auto-generated job number and a type-based icon instantly.

Move it through the work

Drag it across the board as it progresses, edit rich details, drop comments, attach files, and request approval — with a full history and one-click undo behind you.

Report & deliver

Watch live KPIs, save the views you check every day, export to CSV/Excel/JSON for the end-of-year report, and share a direct link to any job.

Built to grow

Local-first today, backend-ready tomorrow.

JobTracker starts as a pure browser app so it's fast and private — and it's designed to progressively connect to real infrastructure as the team needs it.

PHASE 1 · NOW

Local-first

Everything in your browser. IndexedDB for attachments, full export/import, six themes, undo & history.

PHASE 2

API bridge

Opt-in connection to a lightweight REST server bridging to SQLite or PostgreSQL.

PHASE 3

Backend-as-a-service

Supabase / PocketBase for auth, role-based access, and managed file storage.

PHASE 4

Edge sync

WebAssembly databases in the browser that sync automatically when online.
